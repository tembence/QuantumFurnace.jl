Open Quantum System Evolution

In this tutorial, we will walk through the process of finding the steady-state thermal density matrix $\rho_{ss}$ for a system coupled to a thermal bath. The evolution of such a system is governed by the Lindblad master equation:

\[\frac{d\rho}{dt} = -i[H, \rho] + \sum_k \gamma_k \left( L_k \rho L_k^\dagger - \frac{1}{2}\{L_k^\dagger L_k, \rho\} \right)\]

Our goal is to find the state $\rho_{ss}$ where $\frac{d\rho}{dt} = 0$.
